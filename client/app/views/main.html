General

The Deep Space Climate Observatory (DSCOVR) is the planned replacement for the NASA Advanced Composition Explorer (ACE) for monitoring the solar wind at L1. Here, data will be archived for visualization and download. For realtime data, please visit the Space Weather Prediction Center (SWPC).

Space weather instruments

DSCOVR is carrying the following space weather monitoring instruments:

Nick Name Purpose
PlasMag Solar Wind Plasma Sensor and Magnetometer Measure solar wind velocity, magnitude and direction of the solar wind magnetic filed
PHA Pulse Height Analyzer Provide real-time measurement of particle events that may impact DSCOVR's electronics
