General

The Deep Space Climate Observatory (DSCOVR) is replacing NASA's Advanced Composition Explorer (ACE) for monitoring space weather at L1. The data collected by DSCOVR enables forecasters to monitor space weather conditions and supports research investigations. The results of these efforts will be warning and understanding of conditions that could disrupt earth based infrastructure.

Here, we archive, distribute, and provide basic visualization tools for magnetometer and faraday cup measurements. For realtime data, please visit our friends at the Space Weather Prediction Center (SWPC). For EPIC and NISTAR data, please visit NASA's Atmospheric Science Data Center .

Space weather instruments

DSCOVR is carrying the following space weather monitoring instruments:

Nick Name Purpose
PlasMag Solar Wind Plasma Sensor and Magnetometer Measure solar wind velocity, magnitude and direction of the solar wind magnetic filed
PHA Pulse Height Analyzer Provide real-time measurement of particle events that may impact DSCOVR's electronics

FAQ

What is DSCOVR?
DSCOVR is the Deep Space Climate Observatory. See DSCOVR Home .

February 11, 2015.

Where can I access real time solar wind data from DSCOVR?
From NOAA's Space Weather Prediction Center (SWPC).
Where can I access EPIC or NISTAR data?
From NASA's Atmospheric Science Data Center
Why do the time series plots make my computer slow?
The time series plots are resource intensive. A high volume of data and lots of interactivity contribute to this. We reccomend reducing the number of panes, lines, or span of time you have plotted. We have not imposed any limits on the number of panes you may create in the time series under the philosophy that no limit is suitable for everyone's hardware capabilities.
How can I share the plot I have created with someone else?
Just share the URL of the page! If you have created a plot, it can be uniquely identified by the url.
How can I embed a plot I have created with these tools into a presentation?
You must take a screen shot of the plot if you wish to embed the plot in a presentation.
How can I obtain a publication quality plots?
We provide pre-generated plots under visualization/summary. Our site also permits you to download the data associated with any custom visualization that you create, so taht you can generate a relevant plot with your own software if you prefer.
How do I find interesting events to visualize?
We have provided the "find events" module to address this. It is easy to search the entire dataset with this tool.
